Need help choosing your Epicodus track?

Just take this quiz!

You should take the Ruby/Rails track!

Ruby is great for building interactive web applications. It's popular with smaller companies and startups, so if you prefer working with a very small team and getting a product off the ground quickly, it may be a great fit!

You should take the PHP/Drupal track!

You can use PHP to build content management systems and to build out sites for brands, businesses and organizations. Oftentimes, agencies that build websites for clients use PHP.

You should take the Java/Android track!

With Java, you can build Android user interfaces! Like C#, Java is also popular with bigger, enterprise-level businesses. If ou're interested in working for a bigger business, this may be a great fit for you!

You should take the CSS/Design track!

CSS allows you to delve further into making websites look great and function even better. If you enjoy obsessing over the layout and styles of a webpage, then this may be the right track for you. Since you'll be working with designers, it will bring you one step closer to being that person who hangs out with the stylish art types.

You should take the C#/.NET track!

With C# and .NET, you can build internal software for businesses! Big businesses tend to like C#, and the use of .NET is quickly growing.
